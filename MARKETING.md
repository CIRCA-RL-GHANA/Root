## Target Audience Segments

### 1. Urban Professionals (Individual Users)
- Age: 22–40
- Location: Accra, Lagos, Kumasi, Abuja
- Needs: fast P2P payments, reliable rides, convenient shopping
- Key features: GO Wallet, Live rides, Market, QualChat

### 2. Small & Medium Businesses (SMEs)
- Shops, restaurants, delivery services, service providers
- Needs: digital storefront, order management, staff management, disbursements
- Key features: Setup Dashboard, Market seller tools, GO batch payments, Analytics

### 3. Drivers / Delivery Riders
- Gig economy workers
- Needs: steady income, transparent earnings, reliability
- Key features: Live driver view, earnings dashboard, QPoints rewards

### 4. Market Sellers
- Informal traders and small product sellers
- Needs: online presence, easy payment collection, customer reach
- Key features: Market seller profile, product listing, orders management

## Key Metrics to Track

### Acquisition
- App installs (Android, iOS, PWA)
- Onboarding completion rate (phone → first transaction)
- Referral rate (wireId sharing, referral codes)

### Engagement
- Daily / Monthly Active Users (DAU / MAU)
- Transactions per user per month
- Feature adoption: % users who have used GO, Live, Market, QualChat

### Revenue
- Transaction volume (GHS / NGN)
- Platform fee collected (8% of ride fares by default)
- Subscription revenue (Premium / VIP tiers)
- QPoints redemption rate

### Retention
- 7-day, 30-day, 90-day retention cohorts
- Churn by user segment

## Growth Levers

### Referral Programme
- User A invites User B via wireId or link
- Both earn QPoints bonus on User B's first transaction (`RideReferral` entity tracks this)
- Technical status: `RideReferral` entity exists in codebase — referral bonus logic needs product configuration

### QPoints Loyalty
- QPoints earned on every transaction across all modules
- Drives cross-module usage (earn on rides, spend on Market)
- Transferable — social virality ("send your friend some QPoints")

### Subscription Upsell
- Free → Premium: reduced fees, priority ride matching, advanced analytics
- Premium → VIP: custom fee structures, dedicated support, full AI features

### Business Onboarding
- Quick-start wizard (34-screen guided Setup Dashboard)
- Reduces time-to-first-transaction for business accounts
- Each business onboarded brings their customer base to the platform
