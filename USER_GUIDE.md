## Getting Started

### Download the App

Install PROMPT Genie from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

### Create Your Account

1. **Enter your phone number** — We'll send you a 6-digit SMS code.
2. **Verify the OTP** — Enter the code within 5 minutes.
3. **Create a password** — Must be at least 8 characters.
4. **Choose your role** — Individual, Business, or Driver.
5. **Set up your profile** — Add your name and optional photo.
6. **Enable biometrics** — (Optional) Use fingerprint or Face ID for quicker login.
7. **Grant permissions** — Location (for rides), camera (for photos), notifications.

### Log In

- Enter your phone number and password.
- Use biometrics if you enabled it during setup.
- If you forget your password, tap **Forgot Password** to receive an SMS reset code.

## GO Wallet

The GO wallet manages your money on PROMPT Genie.

### Check Your Balance

Open the **GO** tab to see your current balance, recent transactions, and quick actions.

### Top Up

1. Tap **Top Up** on the GO screen.
2. Enter the amount.
3. Choose your payment method: Mobile Money, Bank Card.
4. Follow the provider's payment prompt.
5. Funds appear instantly.

### Send Money

1. Tap **Transfer**.
2. Enter the recipient's **wireId** (e.g. `WR-A1B2C3`) or phone number.
3. Enter the amount and an optional note.
4. Review and confirm.
5. Both parties receive an instant notification.

### Batch Payments

Send to multiple recipients at once:

1. Tap **Batch Payments**.
2. Add recipient wireIds and amounts.
3. Review the total.
4. Confirm with your PIN.

### Download Statement

1. Go to **GO → Statement**.
2. Select a date range.
3. Download as PDF or CSV.

### QPoints

Earn QPoints on every transaction. View your balance and history in **GO → QPoints**. Transfer QPoints to friends or redeem them for discounts.

## Live — Rides & Deliveries

### Book a Ride

1. Open **Live** and tap **Request Ride**.
2. Set your **pickup** location (your current location is detected automatically).
3. Set your **dropoff** destination.
4. Browse available vehicle types (Economy, Standard, Premium).
5. Review the **estimated fare** — includes any surge pricing.
6. Tap **Confirm Ride**.
7. A driver is matched and their location is shown on the map.
8. Share the **4-digit PIN** displayed on screen with your driver before starting.

### During Your Ride

- Track your driver's real-time location on the map.
- See estimated arrival time.
- Tap **SOS** in case of emergency — your location and alert are sent immediately.

### After Your Ride

- Rate your driver (1–5 stars).
- Add optional feedback tags (Clean, Polite, Safe Driving, On Time).
- Payment is deducted from your GO wallet automatically.

### Fare Calculation

Fares are calculated based on distance, time, and current demand. A surge multiplier (up to 3.5×) may apply during peak hours (morning 7–9am, evening 5–8pm) or high-demand periods. The estimated fare is always shown before you confirm.

### Track a Delivery

Active delivery orders appear in **Live → Deliveries**. Tap any active order to see the courier's real-time location.

## Market — Shopping

### Browse Products

Open **Market** to explore products by category or featured listings.

### Search

Use the search bar for instant results. AI-powered search understands natural language — try "red wireless headphones under 200" rather than exact product names.

### Product Page

- View photos, description, price, stock availability.
- Check seller rating and reviews.
- Tap **Add to Cart** or **Buy Now**.

### Checkout

1. Review your cart.
2. Confirm or update your delivery address.
3. Select payment method: GO Wallet, Mobile Money, Card.
4. Tap **Place Order**.
5. You'll receive an order confirmation notification.

### Track Your Order

Go to **Market → My Orders** to see:
- Order status: Pending → Confirmed → Fulfilling → Delivered
- Courier location when your order is in transit

### Returns

1. Open the order and tap **Request Return**.
2. Select the reason and affected items.
3. Submit — the seller reviews your request within 48 hours.
4. If approved, the refund is processed to your GO wallet.

## QualChat — Messaging

### Start a Conversation

1. Open **QualChat**.
2. Tap **New Chat**.
3. Search for the user by name or wireId.
4. Type your message and send.

### Features

- **Read receipts** — double tick when a message is read.
- **Typing indicators** — see when someone is typing.
- **Media** — send images and documents.
- **AI Summary** — tap **Summarise** in a long conversation to get a quick recap.

## APRIL — Finance Planner

### Overview

APRIL helps you manage your finances with a calendar-based view of income and expenses.

### Set a Budget

1. Go to **APRIL → Goals**.
2. Tap **New Goal** and set a target amount and date.
3. APRIL tracks your progress automatically.

### View Spending Insights

APRIL's AI analyses your transaction history and surfaces:
- Spending patterns by category
- Month-over-month comparisons
- Forecasted balance based on spending trends

### Wishlist

Save products from Market to your wishlist. APRIL's AI scores each item against your budget and lets you know when you can comfortably afford it.

## Account & Security

### Change Password

**User Details → Security → Change Password**  
Enter your current password, then your new password twice.

### Enable / Disable Biometrics

**User Details → Security → Biometric Login**

### View Active Sessions

**User Details → Security → Active Sessions**  
Log out of any session you don't recognise immediately.

### KYC Verification

Higher transaction limits require identity verification:

1. Go to **User Details → Verification**.
2. Upload a government-issued ID (passport, national ID, driver's licence).
3. Verification is typically completed within 24 hours.

### Privacy Settings

**User Details → Privacy**

- Control who can see your profile (Everyone / Followers / Nobody)
- Control who can send you messages
- Request a copy of your data
- Request account deletion
